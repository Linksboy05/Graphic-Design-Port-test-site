About Me

I’m Aiden Demers, a graphic designer, esports broadcaster, and collegiate esports player with a deep passion for creative storytelling and competitive gaming. I’ve spent the last several years developing my skills both in front of the screen and behind the scenes. As a graphic designer, I specialize in creating bold, impactful visuals that elevate brands, teams, and events especially within the gaming and esports space. Whether it’s social media graphics, event branding, or motion design, I focus on clean, purposeful design that captures attention and communicates clearly.
