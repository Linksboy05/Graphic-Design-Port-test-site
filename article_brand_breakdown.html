Brand Breakdown: Esports Team Rebrand

Published on March 3, 2026

Project Overview

This breakdown dives into the comprehensive rebranding of a competitive esports team. We'll explore logo evolution, color systems, typography choices, and how each element aligns with the team's identity and target audience.

Logo Evolution

The initial logo concept featured aggressive shapes and sharp edges to convey energy. Through iteration, we simplified the form to a bold monogram that reads well at small sizes and pairs with a dynamic mascot illustration.

Color Palette

The chosen palette blends vibrant orange with deep maroon to evoke excitement and strength. Accent tones of electric blue provide contrast for digital applications and live overlays.

Typography

A custom typeface was developed based on geometric sans-serif foundations. It ensures legibility during fast-paced broadcasts while retaining a unique character.

Application Examples

Mockups include jersey designs, social media posts, and stream overlays. Each application reinforces brand consistency and responds to the esports audience's expectations.

Conclusion

This rebrand aims to position the team as modern and formidable while maintaining flexibility across physical and digital platforms. Its modular design system allows easy updates for future seasons.
