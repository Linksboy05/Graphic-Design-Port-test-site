3D Art and Animation

Showcase of 3D art and animation to create a sense of realism and sense of creativity.

Can

Using Blender, Starting from a cylinder shape and modeling it into a can shape the basics of modeling, i then creating a UV map and leveling it out to make the PNG design created to fit perfecting on the model.

Earth Render

As a test of using Blender, This is a rough render using NASA supplied images and textures taken by their satellite. Moving the light source (Sun) it moves in real time showing that when a side of the planet does not show natural light, the city lights shop up in returning. Replicating how earth really works between the light side half and the dark side half.

Orb

Created a custom Plane which features a rocky terrain including being modeled with high res photos to simulate realism. The main focus is the rotating sphere. The sphere itslef is stuck in place HOWEVER there’s almost an alien like substance surrounding the orb which moves in 2 different directions. The animation can be viewed on insta - bobross5000.
